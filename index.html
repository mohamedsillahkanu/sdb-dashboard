School-Based Distribution (SBD) of ITNs

Summary presentation of SBD

Overview
The School-Based Distribution (SBD) of Insecticide-Treated Nets (ITNs) represents a strategic approach to malaria prevention in Sierra Leone. This initiative leverages the existing educational infrastructure to ensure comprehensive coverage and accessibility of life-saving bed nets to vulnerable populations.
Key Objectives:
• Achieve universal coverage of ITNs in target districts
• Utilize schools as distribution points for community access
• Ensure equitable distribution across rural and urban areas
• Strengthen community engagement in malaria prevention efforts

Conclusion & Recommendations
The School-Based Distribution of ITNs has demonstrated significant success in achieving comprehensive coverage across Bo and Bombali districts. The strategic use of educational infrastructure has proven effective in reaching both rural and urban communities.
